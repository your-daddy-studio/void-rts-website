date: "2026-03-02"

summary: "Six Homeworld-inspired ships replace the old fleet — homing missiles, ion beams, tactical ground grid, custom sci-fi cursor, and reworked UI."
---

- Replaced 5 ship types with 6 Homeworld-inspired ships: Destroyer (flagship), Heavy Cruiser (missiles), Ion Cruiser (beam weapon), Carrier (support capital), Scout (fast recon), Collector (cargo hauler)
- New color system: all ships share neutral gray-blue hull (70%) with team accent colors (30%) — blue friendly, red enemy; Destroyer and Carrier have flashing window lights
- Homing missiles with smoke trails (Heavy Cruiser + Destroyer)
- Sustained ion beam cannon (Ion Cruiser) with charge/fire/cooldown phases — works on ships and asteroids
- Heavy cannons with penetration (Destroyer + Carrier)
- Anti-scout auto-turrets on Carrier
- Tactical ground mesh: blue grid plane with intersection crosses, breathing pulse animation, periodic flash, and radial sweep intro on game start
- Custom sci-fi cursor: CRT-style triangle pointer with slow glow, plus crosshair alternate (Shift+1/2 to switch)
- Reduced from 2 bases per side to 1 friendly + 1 enemy base
- Bottom panel reworked: now shows only selected friendly ships and their targeted enemies instead of all ships
- Max-width cap on HP/hull status bars for cleaner look
- Ships no longer spawn inside base structures
